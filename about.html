About Us

Hi Everyone! My name is Genevieve, Genna for short, and I am the founder and owner of The Realm of Crystals! Ever since I could remember, I have loved and been drawn to Crystals. Whenever I went to the beach or a lake, my Dad and I would search the sandbars for rocks, shells and fossils. But Crystals, in particular, I had a fascination with them. During the 2020 lockdown, I became more interested in the metaphysical. I began learning tarot and all about crystals, how they hold energies, and how people can utilize them to heal and deal with life's ups and downs. Then, one day, I decided to chase my dreams and open a shop named The Realm of Crystals and so 6 months ago I opened my shop. My shop is a realm of its own, filled with magic, healing and tranquillity! Stop by our shop! We have Crystals for everyone and everything!
